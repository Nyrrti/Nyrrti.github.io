About me

Hello!

My name is Elly Hoekstra also known as Nyrrti. I currently live in Friesland where I work as a Digital Artist.

As youngster I’ve always been fascinated with the realm of fantasy. Creating worlds, characters, animals.. to bring imaginary worlds to life.

I’ve drawn many dragons over the years and in time I expanded beyond that and started drawing environments, people or giant buildings. I have a passion for drawing anything related to fantasy.

I'm presently pursuing freelance illustration and developing personal projects.
